The Story

Every monster has a story.
This one has a song.

Deep in the uncharted reaches of the Amazon, where the black water meets a sky it has never learned to trust, there lived a creature — ancient, unknowable, alone. When an expedition ventured into his domain, they dragged him from the lagoon in chains, believing the story was over. It was only beginning.

Sold to a traveling wonder show and billed as the Eighth Wonder of the World, the creature is caged, paraded beneath the circus lights, and reduced from marvel of nature to spectacle of cruelty. But within the fraying canvas of the big top, an unlikely bond ignites — and the creature must discover whether a world that captured him has any room for what he truly is.
