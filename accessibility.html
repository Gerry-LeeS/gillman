For All Audiences

Accessibility

Our Commitment

We believe that every audience member deserves to experience the magic of CREATURE. We are committed to making our production accessible to all patrons, including those with disabilities. We work closely with each of our venue partners to ensure a welcoming and accommodating environment for every guest.

Wheelchair Accessibility

All of our partner venues offer wheelchair-accessible seating locations with clear sightlines to the stage. Companion seats are available adjacent to all accessible locations.

  • Wheelchair-accessible seating at all venues
  • Accessible restrooms at all partner theatres
  • Step-free access routes — please confirm with your venue
  • Service animals welcome at all performances

Audio Description

Select performances feature live audio description for patrons who are blind or have low vision. Please check with your local venue for scheduled audio-described performances.

Captioned Performances

Open-captioned performances display the dialogue, lyrics, and sound effects on screens positioned near the stage. Contact your venue box office for the captioned performance schedule.

Assisted Listening Devices

Assisted listening devices are available free of charge at all partner venues.

  • Available at the venue's front-of-house desk
  • Photo ID may be required as a deposit
  • Neck loops and headsets available

Sensory Information

CREATURE contains the following sensory elements:

  • Theatrical haze and fog effects
  • Strobe lighting effects during select scenes
  • Loud sound effects including thunder, water, and percussion
  • Water mist effects in select seating sections
  • Moments of near-complete darkness

Relaxed Performances

We are committed to offering relaxed performances at select venues, featuring adjusted lighting and sound levels, a relaxed attitude to noise and movement, and designated quiet spaces.
