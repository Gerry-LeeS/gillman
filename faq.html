Frequently Asked Questions

GILLMAN runs approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes, including one 15-minute intermission.

GILLMAN is recommended for ages 10 and up. Children under 4 are not permitted in the theatre. The show contains theatrical fog, strobe effects, and moments of dramatic intensity.

Latecomers will be seated at the discretion of house management during an appropriate break in the performance. We strongly recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before curtain.
